Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (grated)
  • 2 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 1 cup fresh cream
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Mix paneer, potatoes, cornflour, and shape into balls.
  2. Deep fry until golden brown.
  3. Heat oil, saut√© onions, add tomato puree, and spices.
  4. Add fresh cream, mix well, and simmer.
  5. Add koftas to the gravy, garnish, and serve.
