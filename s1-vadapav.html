Vada Pav

⏳ 30 mins
🍽️ Serves: 4
🌶️ Very Spicy
🥦 Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 4 pav (bread rolls)
  • 3 large potatoes (boiled & mashed)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies (chopped)
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp coriander leaves (chopped)
  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, green chilies, and ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Add mashed potatoes, turmeric, salt, and mix well.
  3. Shape into small balls and set aside.
  4. Mix besan, baking soda, water, and salt to make a thick batter.
  5. Dip the potato balls in the batter and deep fry until golden brown.
  6. Slice the pav, spread chutney, place the vada inside, and serve hot.

FAQs

Can I make it less spicy?

Yes! Reduce the green chillies or skip them

How do I keep the vada crispy?

Serve immediately and avoid covering it
