Rajma

⏳ 55 mins
🍽️ Serves: 4
🌶️ Mild Spicy
🥦 Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 1 cup kidney beans (soaked overnight)
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp fresh coriander (chopped)

Steps

  1. Pressure cook the soaked kidney beans with 2 cups of water and a little salt for about 20 minutes.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell disappears.
  5. Add pureed tomatoes and cook until oil separates.
  6. Mix in turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, coriander powder, and salt.
  7. Pour in the cooked kidney beans along with their water and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with rice or roti.

FAQs

Can I make rajma in a regular pot instead of a pressure cooker?

Yes! Coarse rava gives a more textured upma, but fine rava makes it softer.

How do I make it creamier?

Yes, but it will take longer—about 45-60 minutes of boiling until soft.
