Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken (cubed)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tbsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, turmeric, and salt. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat butter in a pan, add chopped onions, and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add tomato puree and cook for 5-7 minutes until thick.
  4. Add marinated chicken and cook until tender.
  5. Sprinkle garam masala, kasuri methi, and mix well.
  6. Pour fresh cream, stir, and simmer for 5 minutes.
  7. Garnish with coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.

FAQs

Can I make this without cream?

Yes! Use milk or yogurt as a substitute.

How do I make it spicier?

Increase the red chili powder or add green chilies
