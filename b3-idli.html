Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup urad dal (black gram)
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Steps

  1. Soak rice and urad dal separately for 6-8 hours.
  2. Grind both to a smooth batter, mix well, and ferment overnight.
  3. Add salt to the fermented batter.
  4. Grease idli molds and pour the batter.
  5. Steam in an idli cooker for 10-12 minutes.
  6. Let cool slightly, then remove and serve with chutney and sambar.

FAQs

What if my idlis are hard?

Ensure proper fermentation and do not over-steam.

Can I make idlis without an idli cooker?

Yes! Use a steamer or a pressure cooker without the whistle.
