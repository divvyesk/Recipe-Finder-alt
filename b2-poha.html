Ingredients

  • 1 cup Poha (flattened rice)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup boiled peas
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Rinse poha under water and drain.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds and let them pop.
  3. Add onions and saut√© until translucent.
  4. Add turmeric, peas, and poha. Mix well.
  5. Season with salt and cook for 2 minutes.
  6. Turn off heat, add lemon juice, and garnish with coriander.
  7. Serve hot!

FAQs

Can I use red poha?

Yes! Red poha is a healthier alternative to white poha.

How to make poha crispier?

Soak poha for a shorter time and let it drain well before cooking.
