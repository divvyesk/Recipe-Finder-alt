Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1/4 cup peas
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Mix flour, oil, and salt, then add water to form a dough. Let it rest.
  2. Heat oil, add cumin seeds, peas, mashed potatoes, and spices. Mix well.
  3. Roll the dough, cut into halves, and shape into cones.
  4. Fill with potato mixture and seal the edges.
  5. Deep fry until golden brown and serve hot.

FAQs

How do I keep the samosas crispy?

Let the dough rest longer and fry on medium heat.

Can I freeze samosas?

Yes! Freeze before frying and cook when needed.
